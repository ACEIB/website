Introduction

Greetings everyone and welcome to 'Ace IB'. Our aim is to share knowledge, valuable tips to help you succeed in the rigorous IB program. We hope to provide our peers with the resources they need to excel in the program. Whether that be the IB exams, the personal project, IAs, EE, CAS and many more.

Our Mission

Here at the Ace IB community, our mission is to provide our fellow students advice to help them succeed in the IB program or even give them recommendations if they wish to pursue the IB program in the future!

We can help with

  • IAs
  • EE
  • CAS

Why Choose Ace IB?

Here at Ace IB, we are second year diploma programme students that understand the hurdles and obstacles each student has to go through. We are here to provide help so that it doesn't have to be as hard for you.
