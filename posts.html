Welcome to Ace IB

Greetings everyone and welcome to the start of ACE IB. This account is managed by fellow IB students who are entering their final year of the Diploma Programme. Our mission is to share knowledge, and valuable tips to help you succeed in the rigorous IB program. We hope to provide our IB peers with the resources they need to excel on the IB exams, the personal project, MYP, IAs and many more!


Fun Fact

According to a American based study done by IB in 2020, 82% of students who go through the Diploma Programme in High-School achieve immediate enrolment into a post secondary school compared to just 66% of the national average. That’s a 16% increase! Source: IBO


The 6 IB Groups

Here are all 6 IB groups, plus the core class known as TOK or Theory of Knowledge. Remember that most schools will only offer a select few courses due to class sizes and other restrictions and only certain courses are available online. Finally, the language taught in the first two groups will be depending on your region and the availability of those classes.


IA Tips and Tricks

Here are some subject-specific Internal Assessment (IA) tips for each IB Diploma Programme Group. An IA is an individual assessment evaluated by the subject teacher with a list of criteria and is usually focused on some subject-related work. Alongside the criteria, samples of the student’s work (oral performances, portfolios, lab reports, and essays) are also submitted to the IB.


Courses

If you are worried about course selection within the IB program, don’t be! IB offers 56 different SL and HL courses across the world. These 56 courses are divided into six groups that are mentioned in our last post. Schools will now offer the select courses, however many alternative options are available, such as combining regular and online courses.
